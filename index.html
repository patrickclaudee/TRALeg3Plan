Coaching Strategy
01

Recovery First

Weeks 1–2 are non-negotiable recovery after Leg 2. Ankle rehab, easy running only. No ego miles.

02

Build the Engine

Weeks 3–10 systematically build from 55→75km. Aerobic base is the foundation for sub-3 durability.

03

Sharpen the Blade

Weeks 8–14 alternate intervals and tempo. One hard session per week, max stimulus, full recovery.

04

Trust the Taper

Weeks 15–16 reduce volume. Legs will feel restless — that's the plan working.

05

Ankle Protocol

Calf raises, resistance band inversions, single-leg balance every S&C day. No exceptions.

06

Nov 8 Vision

Sub-3:00 at 32km = ~5:37/km. That predicts sub-4:00 marathon ability. Stay healthy, let it compound.

🏁 RACE DAY
32km

RACE DAY STRATEGY — SUN SEP 6

Km 0–8: Run by feel, ~5:45–5:50/km. Resist the crowd. Bank comfort, not time.
Km 8–20: Lock into 5:35–5:40/km. Cruise zone. Hold the form.
Km 20–28: This is where the race starts. Maintain. Push 5:30 if feeling good at 24km.
Km 28–32: Empty the tank. Sub-3 is yours if you hit 28km under 2:38.

Nutrition: Carbs every 6km. Salt at 10km & 20km. Caffeine gel at 22km.
Race Shoe: 361 Flame 5 (carbon plated, 38km — perfectly broken in). Save it for race day only.
