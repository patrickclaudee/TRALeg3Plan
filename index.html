Coaching Strategy
01

Recovery First

Weeks 1–2 are non-negotiable recovery after Leg 2. Ankle rehab, easy running only. No ego miles.

02

Build the Engine

Weeks 3–10 systematically build from 55→75km. Aerobic base is the foundation for sub-3 durability.

03

Sharpen the Blade

Weeks 8–14 alternate intervals and tempo. One hard session per week, max stimulus, full recovery.

04

Trust the Taper

Weeks 15–16 reduce volume. Legs will feel restless — that's the plan working.

05

Ankle Protocol

Calf raises, resistance band inversions, single-leg balance every S&C day. No exceptions.

06

Nov 8 Vision

Sub-3:00 at 32km = ~5:37/km. Stay healthy, let it compound.
